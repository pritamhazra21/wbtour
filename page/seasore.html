Sea-sore Travel

Digha Beach of West Bengal is quite famous amongst the locals and is also one of the most visited beaches. The beach is divided into two parts including Old Digha Beach and New Digha Beach. The Old Digha Beach has several souvenir stalls and food shacks whereas New Digha Beach is popular amongst the ones wanted to spend some quality time away from crowd. It is one of the top beaches in West Bengal where the beach lovers can spend some really amazing time. Tajpur Beach is an untouched beach in West Bengal and is located in the east of Midnapore which is a tranquil place.
